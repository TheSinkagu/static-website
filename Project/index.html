About this Fragrance

Oajan by Parfums De Marly is a warm and spicy fragrance that captures the essence of oriental luxury. It is designed to evoke a sense of opulence and sophistication, blending rich spices with sweet and woody notes to create a captivating scent experience. Considered a very classy fragrance and only for those with a a sophisticated taste.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top Notes: Cinnamon, Honey, Osmanthus
  • Mid Notes: Benzoin, Labdanum, Ambergris, Artemisia
  • Base Notes: Vanilla, Tonka, Musk, Patchouli

Longevity and Sillage

This amazing fragrance is known for lasting 8-12 hours in Longevity. And on top of the longevity being well above average. This fragrance leaves a very nice cinnamon roll/french toast type of scent trail.
